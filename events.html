Our Role in KIITFEST

KIIT FEST is a grandiose annual techno-cultural event of KIIT University. This mega fest hosts numerous events where a horde of students from various colleges across the country,along with the students of our university , assimilate energy, enthusiasm, gusto and brio.

The School of Electronics Engineering is highly reputed to organize various innovative technical events during the fest which not only amaze people but also opens a wide door of opportunity to enhance one’s skills and learn various new techniques.

KIIT ROBOTICS SOCIETY feels proud that majority of it’s members form an integral part to organize these events along with several other dedicated students from the university. Following are the events that star the fest at the School of Electronics Engineering :
