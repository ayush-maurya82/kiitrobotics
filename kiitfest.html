Next

Roadrunner

A thrilling track with unexpected twists, turns and challenging obstacles which would test your bot to it’s limits. Does your bot make through ?

The infuriating race of manual bots, in this event two bot race through an innovative and highly complex track full with obstacles of all sorts to delay the efficiency of the bot as:

Points are calculated based on time and obstacles faced.

Next

Mirror Maze

Love challenges ? With hundred of mirrors covering the track, this event is bound to challenge your vision and control to the fullest.

Trapped between numerous mirrors the very bot that enters the maze , turns into it’s own enemy. As you go deep unto the maze and race through the tunnels of multiple reflections , you are either awarded with points or may also end up losing points and the path with no way out.

Next

Line Crawler

Is your autonomous bot capable of making through the advanced line following track fast enough?

An event where even the best don’t find their path correctly. With efficient code and right calibrations one can race through the track or may even lose track . The participants are required to build a line follower bot which would be capable of detecting an obstacle on this way and sense the presence of the colour on the track that lies ahead.

Next

Robowar

The heart of the tech-fest . The most exciting and adventurous technical event. Among the clash of scary weapons can your bot survive?

Design and construct an approximately 30 kg remote controlled bot that would fight other bots until one of them defeats all other in the arena.

Next

Treasure Trove

An event where you go and find the hidden treasures in a treasure island. Are you ready to create from the scratch ?

Hunt for the treasures hidden anywhere in and around the campus,with only the provided clue that would guide you through and help find the designated component .After successfully collecting all the items, create something with them that takes people by amazement. Simple yet tricky, this event may earn you an innovative project in no time!

Next

Innovation Challange

Innovation is what keeps mankind going. So are you innovative enough?

So much of advancement, yet there prevails problems in almost spheres of existence that always show up their ugly face in the day to day life .The participants of this event are encouraged to invest their young innovative minds to come up with suitable solutions to problems that exist in the world. Anyone with the will to create something new and effective for the good cause of the society is welcomed .
